Magnificent cathedral throngs for photographs you can buy them or you take your own each window each vista

You do not care to see my photograph of the paving stone I stumbled on looking at the spire.

Poetry of Robert Lyle Temple, 1928-2018 (oldbob, dad, pa, uncle bob, popka, grampa bob, the professor)
