Poems of My Father

Patterns of Thought

Patterns of thought a maple tree top branches gone all bare against a sky of clear and cloud in singing air without a stir the morning comes so clean with something secret in the scene the birdnest in lower branches still with leaves common enough the sparrow flies and chirps and grieves

Poetry of Robert Lyle Temple, 1928-2018 (oldbob, dad, pa, uncle bob, popka, grampa bob, the professor)
