Poems of My Father

Big Idea

Your hand just now that rested on my arm brought me images of love or is that word too big

a more specific word is trust, for you can rest your hand on me any time you want to. Still a closer word is touch, but what filled my head importantly was hand-on-arm which soon gave way to still another word: hand.

Poetry of Robert Lyle Temple, 1928-2018 (oldbob, dad, pa, uncle bob, popka, grampa bob, the professor)
