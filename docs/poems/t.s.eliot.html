Poems of My Father

T S Eliot

He was so matter of fact telling the news about the universe. When that got old, the universe, still young, had nothing much for anyone to tell about. Later, when, like Einstein. we came to understand him, the truths had changed. Still there is power in what we thought he knew; one head challenging the galaxies to give up pictures of their skeletons.
