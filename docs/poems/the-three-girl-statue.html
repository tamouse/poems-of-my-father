Poems of My Father

The Three Girl Statue

here and in Paris happy they could get away

quicker than birds they dart about the garden admiring the people ticketed, dressed up, talking, far from what they are.

Poetry of Robert Lyle Temple, 1928-2018 (oldbob, dad, pa, uncle bob, popka, grampa bob, the professor)
