Poems of My Father

Pray

If you want to go ahead and pray I’ll wait out here and watch the clouds a while, it won’t make any difference today.

The piper has come by to get his pay you know pay the piper don’t you? Smile if you want to, go ahead and pray

Poetry of Robert Lyle Temple, 1928-2018 (oldbob, dad, pa, uncle bob, popka, grampa bob, the professor)
