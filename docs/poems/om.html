Poems of My Father

Om

to say a name to say to say a name until the word is something more than name the sound the sound repeating of that name until the thing is found that is the name

the word unheard the contemplated name the call the all in one the toral name the plea the sweet persuasive signal name but more and more than any other name

Poetry of Robert Lyle Temple, 1928-2018 (oldbob, dad, pa, uncle bob, popka, grampa bob, the professor)
