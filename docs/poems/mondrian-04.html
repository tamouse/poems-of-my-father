Mondrian IV

shattering that what anyone can see through is so readily forgotten but one moment before everything gave way someone took me for an entrance

but there is never time one short glimpse for gettng ready crowding crystals in the right places to make the design important at least noticeable not to be disappointing

Poetry of Robert Lyle Temple, 1928-2018 (oldbob, dad, pa, uncle bob, popka, grampa bob, the professor)
