Question my meaning no more than to ask of the gambler what is gaming, or inquire of the tree why eastward leaning, of the flying fish its task, or what of light and painful flaming of the fire.

Poetry of Robert Lyle Temple, 1928-2018 (oldbob, dad, pa, uncle bob, popka, grampa bob, the professor)
