Poems of My Father

Regatta for Thirteen and Under

He fights in flight waves slapping slipping from shore balancing directions flag to keel.

Wise to the ways of wind light touches white on blue he paints the warmth of summer with his sails.

Poetry of Robert Lyle Temple, 1928-2018 (oldbob, dad, pa, uncle bob, popka, grampa bob, the professor)
