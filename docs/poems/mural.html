Mural

this letter is a mural runs along the wall across the window right on the glass write on

one long line now and then it runs off sidelines sidebars shaped locutions of nonsense and misguided sagacity

forgets itself and runs up to the ceiling falls to the floor trundles along out the door up on the wall again out in the hallway leading to the elevator
