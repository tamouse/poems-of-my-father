Poems of My Father

Out of the Waves

give them today today give them today just one more day we’ll see about the morning in the morning

give them today one cycled sun some hours won we’ll see about the morning in the morning

give them today a certain bed a body fed their fear unsaid give them today their daily bread we’ll see about the morning in the morning

Poetry of Robert Lyle Temple, 1928-2018 (oldbob, dad, pa, uncle bob, popka, grampa bob, the professor)
