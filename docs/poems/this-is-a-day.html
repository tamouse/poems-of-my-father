Poems of My Father

This is a Day

this is a day the lord has made filled with a holy faith and grace into destruction we have strayed with a shadowed terror we must face

we may well vanish without a trace this is a day the lord has made with a shadowed terror we must face what land did we so swift invade

We’ve in what devil’s thicket strayed with a shadowed terror we must face this is a day the lord has made what demon chose we to embrace

with a shadowed terror we must face such foolish victims, fools out-played in burned out times in a desert place this is a day that the lord has made

Poetry of Robert Lyle Temple, 1928-2018 (oldbob, dad, pa, uncle bob, popka, grampa bob, the professor)
