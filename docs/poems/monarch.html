Poems of My Father

Monarch

As the Monarch butterfly in Minnesota Remembers Monterey and a lost forest in Mexico As the lamphreys swim from Latvia to Labrador Longer is the reaching with each season So is the long, long way we come from you and I.

Poetry of Robert Lyle Temple, 1928-2018 (oldbob, dad, pa, uncle bob, popka, grampa bob, the professor)
